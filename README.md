# 🌍 Global Electricity Mix & Consumption Analysis

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** that analyzes over **25 years of global electricity generation and consumption data** across **143 countries**. The project enables policymakers, analysts, and stakeholders to evaluate renewable energy adoption, fossil fuel dependency, regional energy trends, and the financial implications of energy transitions.

---

# 📌 Project Overview

This project provides a comprehensive analysis of global electricity generation and consumption using historical energy data collected from official international organizations.

Approximately **95% of the dataset is compiled from official government and international energy sources**, ensuring high reliability for analytical and policy evaluation purposes.

The dashboard is designed to support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations and regional benchmarking.

---

# 🎯 Objectives

- Analyze global electricity generation trends over the past 25+ years.
- Compare renewable and fossil fuel electricity generation across regions.
- Study country-wise electricity consumption patterns.
- Benchmark regional energy mixes.
- Evaluate the financial impact of energy transition policies.
- Support sustainability and strategic energy planning.
